Projects

Indian Railways' Mobile Apps

Indian Railways has launched several mobile applications that assist passengers with station-related navigation. The NTES app provides real-time train schedules and platform information.

IRCTC Next-Gen E-Ticketing System

While mainly a ticketing platform, IRCTC’s Next-Gen portal includes features that help passengers plan their station arrival and navigation.

SMART Stations Program

Under the SMART Station initiative, Indian Railways has aimed to modernize major railway stations with digital infrastructure that includes LED signages, digital kiosks, and real-time tracking systems.

Digital India Initiatives

Several projects under Digital India aim to make public services more accessible, and this includes railway station navigation enhancements. Features like QR codes, AI-based voice assistants, and AR/VR-based navigation

National Train Enquiry System

The Rail Navik app, launched by the Eastern Railways, is designed to provide real-time information on train platforms and station amenities like restrooms, food stalls, and ticket counters.

Why Choose Us?

Seamless Navigation Experience

We understand how overwhelming it can be to navigate busy railway stations, especially during peak hours. Our intuitive platform offers real-time guidance, interactive maps, and step-by-step directions to help you find platforms, services, and facilities with ease. Say goodbye to confusion and last-minute rushes!

Comprehensive Station Information

From restrooms and waiting areas to food courts and luggage services, we provide detailed information about all the essential facilities available at each station. Our platform helps you locate exactly what you need, so you can focus on a comfortable and convenient travel experience.

24/7 Passenger Assistance

Need help during your journey? Our dedicated virtual assistants and support system are here for you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ensuring you have access to all the information and help you need, no matter when or where you're traveling. Whether you're navigating a crowded station or looking for real-time train updates in the middle of the night, we’ve got you covered.
